# How to Manage Product Returns Without Losing Money — Small Business India Guide

Returns are an unavoidable part of retail — especially once you go online. The key is a return policy and process that protects your margins while keeping customers satisfied.

## Return Policy Principles That Protect Your Business

- **Shorter return windows reduce return rates** — 7 days performs better than 30 days for profitability
- **Restocking fees on change-of-mind returns** — recovers handling cost
- **Photos of delivered items as proof** — prevents false damage claims
- **Exchange-only policy for certain categories** — reduces refund cash outflow
