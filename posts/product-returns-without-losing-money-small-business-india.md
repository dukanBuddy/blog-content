  <h1 class="blog-title">How to Manage Product Returns Without Losing Money — Small Business India Guide</h1>
  <p class="blog-subtitle">Returns are an unavoidable part of retail — especially once you go online. The key is a return policy and process that protects your margins while keeping customers satisfied.</p>
</div>

<div class="blog-section">
  <h2>Return Policy Principles That Protect Your Business</h2>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Shorter return windows reduce return rates</strong> — 7 days performs better than 30 days for profitability</li>
    <li><strong>Restocking fees on change-of-mind returns</strong> — recovers handling cost</li>
    <li><strong>Photos of delivered items as proof</strong> — prevents false damage claims</li>
    <li><strong>Exchange-only policy for certain categories</strong> — reduces refund cash outflow</li>
  </ul>
